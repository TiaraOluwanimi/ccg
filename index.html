Welcome Message From The CCG Board

Networking to Grow

People of God !

It has been an eventful year. The world has been physically distant and we all have had to do without the love and warmth from hugs, visits, and play.

Many of us have used the opportunity to acquire various, certificates, attend career talks and self-improvement webinars. We've definitely made the best of our 'lockdown" season, but hey ! we can't close the year boring. We want to make the most of what is left and that is why we are having the CCG Christmas edition

Between November 1st and Dec 25th, we will be having the final session of the Covenant Community Groups for the year 2020. It is a special ending hence it is tagged : the Christmas ðŸŽ„ Edition.

It is a season of love, joy, and friendship; so we want to make the best of it by connecting to new friends and sharing love.

The prime goal for everyone participating is to consciously create new networks, and make new friends that you will end the year on a high with and that will help launch you into the new year.

This season will be non-intensive and not too formal so that we can all let down our hairs, enjoy ourselves, and make new friends.

Success for us is that new business partnerships will be born, new jobs will be created, new marriages will form, new political alliances will be built new money will be made and new prayer partners will emerge.

There will be 54 themes where people that have similar interests will participate and whilst there will be theme-specific conversations, we will have general sessions, that will end with one exciting year-end networking session. This Christmas, we there will be dull moments.

Groups will have deep conversations, pockets of lunches and dinners, book reading sessions, fitness exercises, prayer for the new year, and so on.

If you are committed to meeting new exciting people, or you are looking for that next level connection then join us.

Each group will have two main programs.

1) On Networking, Making, and Keeping Friends. 2) A Christmas themed event with group-specific conversations

We will give our reports in pictures, so get the cameras clicking

Let's show the world ðŸŒŽ how to bond! Let everyone know that something is up with this Covenant Nation.

Warm regards,
Obinna Ukachukwu for the CCG Board
