Young Men’s Community Group

The Young Men’s Community is an initiative of TCN aimed at raising and mentoring young men to rise up to their position and responsibility as men in society in accordance with God’s word. The community focuses its activities on Faith, Leadership, Family, Purpose, Productivity and Evangelism

Our Weekly Activities

Daily Activity

  • The community group holds daily review of its selected text on WhatsApp, discussing and drawing lessons from the day’s read.

Mentoring Sessions

  • Week 3, 6 & 9 Mentoring sessions with model men holds three times in a semester; 3 rd , 6 th and 9 th weeks respectively.

Group Details

  • Expected Target Members Young men (Preferably single) ages 21 – 35. A total of 150 members in 5 subgroups of 30 participants each.
  • Previous SpeakersPrevious speakers include Tayo Banigbe and Akinpelu Adeniran
