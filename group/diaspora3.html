DIASPORA GROUP

The Diaspora group is a group of Christian professionals resident in various countries outside Nigeria with the common goal of bonding and knowledge exchange. The group membership cuts across different fields of professional endeavours and industries. The demographic of the group is quite diverse. Last semester the group focussed on the following aspects:
Financial Literacy with focus on Wealth Creation and Management
Family: Focus on Raising Godly Children irrespective of location
Career Growth: How to Navigate through a foreign work environment using productivity, personal branding and visibility

The Governor - Adeyinka Jegede

Adeyinka Jegede is a multi-talented and highly skilled business analyst, data privacy expert, GRC and ISMS consultant with over 16 years experience providing enterprise leadership in the Financial and IT service Industries. He is a strategic leader versed in managing large teams across nationalities and cultures. He has spent the last decade developing middle managers and helping them advance their careers. His leadership training program produced over 400 Managers at one of Africa’s biggest banks. Proven with ability to employ technology in solving complex problems and provide extraordinary results. He played a key role in the implementation of the Biometric Verification Number (BVN) system in Nigeria and served on several committees in the financial service Industry in Africa. Adeyinka is a first class graduate and scholar of the University of Lagos from where he obtained an MBA in international finance. He currently leads the IT Governance team for Better Impact Inc across North America, Europe, Australia and New Zealand
