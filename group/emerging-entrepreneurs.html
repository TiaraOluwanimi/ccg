Emerging Entrepreneurs

This is a Group of Employed professionals who currently run a side business and those who plan to transition from paid employment to become full-time Entrepreneurs.

If you want Greater Focus, Direction AND Clarity as a new or upcoming Entrepreneur this is what you need!

You'll have immediate access to a network of people at different levels of their Entrepreneurial journey. Upon completion, you should have a clear path to business ownership. If you or someone you know is dreaming about self-employment , a side hustle but stalled due to insufficient information, network, focus, direction and conviction, the tools you require for making better, more informed decisions can be found here.

Governor 2 - Ernest Eso

Ernest Eso is a Governor in the CCG, he coordinates several Leaders within the CCG structure. Ernest is a Senior Business Development and Sales professional with experience in IT, Telecoms, Consulting, Logistics and Financial services sectors. He has worked with and consulted for some organizations such as AIRTEL, MTN, Nigerian Breweries and Wapic Insurance in various capacities.
