Millennials at Work

The Millennials at Work Group is a group for high-flying young professionals in the corporate world. The group’s demography comprises millennials aged between 23 and 40. The group provides a haven for millennials with a desire to better network, manage up, be more visible at work and generally achieve their potential in the work and market place.

The Governor - Nkem Anorue

Nkem Anorue is a Offshore & Metocean Poject Surveyor with over 7 years experience in the Energy Sector covering operations, strategy, planning and geoinformation management. He holds a B.Sc from the University of Lagos. Nkem has being a member of The Covenant Nation for over 8 years and was a member of the pilot Millennials at Work Group. Since then, he has co-led other ‘Millennials at Work’ groups before being appointed as a governor in the last semester. Nkem is very passionate about personal finance & spends his free time coaching others on the journey towards financial independence & freedom.
