COVENANT PARENTS COMMUNITY GROUP

Covenant Parents Community is a group of Intentional Parents who want to gain more knowledge on how to raise Godly children: and also build helpful relationships with one another.

The Governor - Dr Titilayo Adesanmi

Dr TITILAYO ADESANMI, medical doctor of twenty-five years’ experience is a practising Paediatrician who has been involved in child health care in various capacities in faith-based hospitals, the public and private sectors in various geopolitical zones in Nigeria. She is a member of the West African College of Physicians and a fellow of the National Postgraduate Medical College in Paediatrics. Recently, she co-authored some chapters in a major text book used in teaching paediatrics at both the undergraduate and post-graduate levels in Nigeria.

She is passionate about improving the quality of child care at all levels. Therefore she is a regular resource person on various child health matters on television, radio and for several schools in Lagos. She also hosts an Instagram page (drtitiadesanmi) where she regularly provides all child care givers with useful tips on child care.

Presently she is the medical director at Lifefort Children’s Medical Centre, Surulere. She is happily married with three children.

Our Weekly Activities

Week 1 - 3

  • Week 1 Inaugural meeting.

    What is parenting?
    Parenting skills [The 3 Ws: What are these? Why do we need them? When to use specific skills (situation awareness)?
    Understanding your child: The relationship between child developmental stages and parenting.
  • Week 2 Project 1: Understanding your child: The relationship between child developmental stages and parenting.

    Each participant is to explore the developmental stage that his/her child is in right now based on the child’s age and identify the following:
    Peculiarities of that stage
    Challenges confronting the child at that age
    The impact of (a) & (b) on the parent
    What parenting style(s) is/are most helpful for that developmental stage

    Each participant is to source for information on this topic above via books, electronic media, the bible.

    Discussion: Every participant is expected to share their findings on this topic with the entire group at this point. The CG leader of each group moderates this discussion and summarises the lessons learnt in this execise. This summary is then posted on the whatsapp group for all to archive, especially those who may have missed the meeting.
  • Week 3 Seminar 1 (with Q & A based on topics discussed):
    Connecting with your child
    What does this mean?
    Why do you need to connect with your child?
    How do you connect with your child?
    Emotional intelligence in parenting (know your child)
    The love languages

Week 4 - 6

  • Week 4 Seminar 2 (with Q & A based on topics discussed):
    Parent-Child communication:
    The power of words
    The power of listening
    Recognising the power tussle between you and your child
    Effective communication
    Building life-long healthy relationship with your children.
  • Week 5 Project 2: Parenting and child discipline: Each participant is to source for information on the topics above via books, electronic media, the bible.

    Discussion: Do you need to discipline your child?
    Why? When?
    How? (types/methods of discipline)
    Age-appropriate discipline
    Out-sourcing child discipline
    Conflict resolution (parent-child conflict, sibling-child conflict, Others-child e.g. school teacher conflicts)
    Every participant is expected to share their findings on this topic with the entire group at this point.
  • Week 6 Project 3: Parenting the teenager in the 21st century:
    Dos and Donts
    Understanding your teenaged son/daughter
    Alert line (situational awareness)
    Red line (danger signs)
    Technology and parenting the teenager (includes screen time and the use of devices and social media)
    School options (day vs boarding, co-ed vs same-sex sch)
    Discussion: Each participant is expected to share their findings on this topic with the entire group at this point.
    What will you change in parenting your teenager fropm now on?

Week 7 - 8

  • Week 7 Seminar 3: Equipping your child for life (with Q & A based on topics discussed):
    Building your child’s self esteem (how?)
    Introducing your child to God and helping them to build a personal relationship with God.
    Imparting Godly nature to your children
    Character building e.g. anger mgt, emotional intelligence in the child, good manners, family values
    Discussion: Question and answer session
  • Week 8 General Discussion:
    Challenges confronting a parent in these times
    Solutions to these challenges
    Achieving and sustaining work-life-balance and successful Godly parenting
    Home-schooling versus school-schooling?
    General Q &A.
    Finding support.
    Evaluation & Feedback
    Corporate prayers.
  • Expected Target Members Men and women who are already parents and desire to improve our parenting skills and also desire to help other like-minded parents to improve their parenting skills.
