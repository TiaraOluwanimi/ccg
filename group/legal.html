The Governor - Israel Aye

Israel is the Senior Partner, Energy and Commercial Transactions at Primera Africa Legal and the Founder of Mylawlegal.com, a Virtual Platform for Legal Services. Israel has extensive commercial and legal knowledge of the Oil and Gas business in Nigeria. In addition to his role at Primera Africa Legal, he is a Director at the Aspen Energy Nigeria (Energy Services and Consulting) Group and faculty member of the International Law Institute- African Centre of Excellence (ILI-ACLE). Israel has been named by Who’s Who Legal annual ratings as one of the world’s leading expert in energy and natural resources legal practice consecutively since 2012. Israel was called to the Nigerian Bar in 1993 and holds Bachelor of Laws, LL. B (Hons), Accredited Mediator, Centre for Effective Dispute Resolution U.K. (CEDR) and a Member (Mci.Arb), Chartered Institute of Arbitrators, U.K, (Nigerian Chapter).
