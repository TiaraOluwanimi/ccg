The Governor - Olanrewaju Olamoposi Ogunkunle

Olanrewaju Olamoposi Ogunkunle is a young entrepreneur with a passion for the application of information technology solutions. He is the CEO and Co-Founder at Revocube Technologies: a solution-driven company solving socio-economic problems and surmounting challenges. Prominent Revocube solutions are Schoolcube.net, Classcube.online, Examcentre.ng and SchoolTeller.ng Olanrewaju has also volunteered to play key roles in several Community Development programmes. He is on the advisory board of several NGO’s and initiatives that seek to empower communities with training and mentorship in various ICT skills and entrepreneurship.
