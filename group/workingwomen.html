The Governor - Ngozi Akinyele

Ngozi Akinyele is currently the Head Strategic Brand Management at Union Bank Nigeria where she oversees advertising, brand experience, segment marketing, partnerships, and brand consistency across physical and digital touchpoints. Prior to this, she worked as a Transformation Lead, alongside the Bain & Company team supporting the Bank’s successful turnaround. With a background in Management Consulting at KPMG, Ngozi has led various engagements across public and private organisations, covering Strategy, Retail & Channels, Human Resources, Operations & Technology and Change Management. An alumnus of University of Lagos (BSc Economics) and a member of the Nigerian Institute of Public Relations, Ngozi is a speaker, mentor, wife and mother of two precious daughters.
