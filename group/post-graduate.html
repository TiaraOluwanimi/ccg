Post Graduate Students Community Group

The Covenant Nation Post Graduate Students Community Group is the place to be if you desire to get all relevant information and support as you proceed on your Post-Graduate journey: Master’s or doctorate, intending to study or on-going study. From available international and local scholarships to part-time studies, we provide the platform for peer-to-peer mentoring, learning, research and external guidance as required.

Governor 1 - Eyitayo Ogunyemin

Eyitayo Ogunyemi is a broadly experienced SME-centric legal practitioner, business advisory consultant and a founding partner at Law Accent. He started his law career at the prestigious Falana and Falana’s Chambers where some of his engagements included high ranking litigation cases which cut across secured and unsecured credit transactions, labour relations, arbitration and mediation, fundamental human rights, etc. His versatility in other spheres of law earned him the status of Deputy Head of the Property Unit- a position which he held until December 2015 when he resigned to start LAW ACCENT. He currently heads the business, investment advisory and litigation arms of the Firm. A prolific writer, he is the author of several articles including his business series. Eyitayo has carved a niche for himself as a plain writing expert who makes the complex simple- particularly as it relates to helping businesses to understand how the law operates. Eyitayo is presently a researcher (PhD-in view) at the Nigerian Institute of Advanced Legal Studies (NIALS) where he is researching on adopting plain English language in communicating Legal Documents.

Governor 2 -Anthony Pogbe

Anthony Pogbe is a graduate of Materials & Metallurgical Engineering from University of Lagos. With over 20 years of exciting and unbroken work experience in project management and delivery, Anthony has a deep insight into planning and execution of diverse projects. His excellent communication and interpersonal skills have been brought to fore in the successful completion of several multimillion-dollar projects. As a certified risk facilitator, Anthony facilitates projects and business risks workshops and develops risk management plans. Anthony is a member of Toastmasters International, an organization that empowers individuals to become more effective communicators and leaders. Having completed the Dynamic Leadership and Effective Coaching educational programs, he is a recipient of the prestigious awards of “Competent Communicator” and “Competent Leader” from Toastmaster International. He is communication and leadership coach with a considerable number of mentees under his tutelage. Anthony is currently the President of DeltaAfrik Toastmasters Club having served in the capacity of Vice President Education. Anthony coordinated The Covenant Community Post Graduate Group as a leader for one semester and as a Governor for another semester. He also coordinated the covenant nation working singles group. He is the co-founder of Blossom Food Products, a brand that is fast becoming a household name in the city of Lagos, Nigeria. He is also the founder of ArtisanHub, an on-demand service company that connects well-profiled artisan to customers for exquisite service delivery.
