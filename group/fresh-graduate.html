Fresh Graduates Community Group

The Covenant Nation Fresh Graduates Community Group seeks to provide a Platform for mentoring and career guidance for fresh graduates who are preparing for the workplace. The community affords members the opportunity to develop necessary skills to make them attractive to prospective employers as well as planning for the future.
