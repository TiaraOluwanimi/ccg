Events and Mentoring Community Groups

The events industry is a multi-billion-naira sector and Nigerians are generally known for their social lives – we are happy people. This is a group for all players along the event ecosystem; including event and venue managers, location owners, providers of services including MCs/comperes, ushering, decorators, lighting, sound system, photo & video, rentals (chair, table, stage, etc.), catering & drinks, etc. The group cuts across individuals and registered businesses who are looking for opportunities to network, scale up their business, build a profitable relationship, and get a bigger pie from the multi-billion naira industry.
