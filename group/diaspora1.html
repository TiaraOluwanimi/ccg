DIASPORA GROUP

A group set up to drive community spirit with TCN Members (or followers of Pastor Poju) who have relocated from their home country and live in various countries across the world. As a community we discuss, share ideas and have experts speak to us about issues that are common to us as immigrants : Investments and Wealth Creation, Parenting in the Diaspora, Immigration, building and progressing in your career as an immigrant etc

The Governor - Anu Sanya

Anu Sanya is a digital transformation executive with expertise in defining, developing digital products and Managing Digital Projects for FMCGs, Breweries and Beverages, Not for Profits etc. After 6 years as a Portfolio Manager within a Pan African Commercial Bank, Anu made the switch to the digital technology space within a foremost Data and Marketing Company delivering digital marketing campaigns and Digital Solutions for fortune 500 companies across key regions in Africa. Anu Sanya lives in Scotland, United Kingdom. Right after a Post Graduate Degree Program she fell in Love with the city of Edinburgh (one of the happiest cities in the UK) and began living there. Her current focus is on managing products/projects and gaining expertise in Artificial Intelligence solutions for Not For Profits organizations in Scotland/UK and growing her Wedding Digital Service Business - a wedding registry for newlyweds in Nigeria and the UK. She is always happy to give a tour of the city to anyone who wants to visit.
