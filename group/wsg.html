Work and Study Group

At Work and Study Group, we use a number of tools and resources to help members build and strengthen the capacity to achieve their academic pursuits without hampering their professional goals. In addition to acquiring positive behavioral changes to improve their work and study, members will also be able to team up with other members as accountability buddies in order to hasten the development of various soft skills. This semester, the broad themes are Examination Groundwork, Career Visibility and Networking. It is another semester for our members to become more energized, fired-up and creative.

The Governor - Olayinka Oyetunji

Olayinka Oyetunji is happily married and blessed with three delightful kids. She has over 12 years experience in brokering mergers & acquisitions, valuations, financial modeling, due diligence as well as infrastructure and public sector advisory. She works with EY as a Manager in Strategy and Transactions.

Olayinka is a charterholder of the CFA Institute, a rigorous examination she completed as a mother of three whilst working as a full time professional. She is also a Chartered Accountant and a Chartered Stockbroker.

She is a volunteer at Gender Diversity of the CFA Society Nigeria and a member of Covenant Capital. Olayinka is also a CFA lecturer at New Frontiers Learning Center and teaches finance courses at other tuition centers. She is the Convener of an initiative called C.L.E.A.Nwitholayinka; a platform that uses storytelling to re-energize and inspire people.

She loves to write, read and meet people. Her six-word story is 'Believe and Pursue, Everything is Possible’. She is looking forward to meeting and welcoming new members to the Work and Study Group.
