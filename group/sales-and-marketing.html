SALES AND MARKETING COMMUNITY

The Sales Community is a group of professionals working in the fields of Business-to-business (B2B) and Business-to-consumer (B2C) sales. It includes professionals in relationship management, field sales, business development, real estate, account management, customer service, and other sales-related roles who are looking to sharpen their sales techniques, broaden their networks and advance in their careers. The marketing community is a group of professionals working in the marketing, communications , and advertising industry. They include professionals in brand management, digital marketing, creative agencies, media agencies, consumer insights, and activation agencies looking to interact with one another and share insights that will advance their careers.
