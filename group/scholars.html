Healthcare Professional Community Group

The Covenant Nation Healthcare Community Group comprises individuals who work, own businesses, provide services or are investors in the broad scope in the Healthcare industry. Our activities are centered around networking, building collaborations, personal and industry leadership and developing the critical skills to bring the much-needed transformation to the healthcare industry.

Governor 1 - Dr ‘Kunle Adeyemi-Doro

Dr ‘Kunle Adeyemi-Doro, is a graduate of medicine from the College of Medicine University of Lagos and did his postgraduate training at the Lagos University Teaching Hospital (LUTH) in Diabetes & Endocrinology. He has spent the past 11yrs in the pharmaceutical industry first in Novo Nordisk a multi-national pharmaceutical company specializing in diabetes therapies as medical advisor and rose to be the Medical Affairs and Global Projects manager for Sub-Saharan Africa. He later worked for 5yrs as the country manager of Mylan Laboratories a US based pharmaceutical company, responsible for their Nigerian & Ghanaian operations.
Currently he is the CEO of Medical Entrepreneurs Ltd. a Healthcare Consulting organization focused on healthcare digital transformation and entrepreneurship development amongst healthcare professionals.
A passionate trainer & speaker, he is a member of the John Maxwell Team and has been a guest speaker on ‘Moments with Mo’ and Pharma-health TV. He also serves on the board of Solid Foundation Teens and youth ministry and was the 2013-2015 PTA Chairman of Saint Saviours School Ebute-Meta.
He is married to Titilola, a development consultant and project manager and they are blessed with four children Akinoluwa, Kikunoluwa, Adeoluwa and Iteoluwa

Governor 2 - Dr Lawumi Oluborode

Dr Lawumi Oluborode -Is the Executive Manager of SQHN and Healthcare Quality professional, who has had trainings by the Joint Commission International in the US on Standards, Quality Improvement and Accreditation. She is an accomplished, versatile and experienced Patient Safety expert with trainings from John Hopkins. She has the experience and in-depth knowledge of data collection and analysis of healthcare indicators for continuous Quality Improvement. She was a key member of the team that worked towards Lagoon Hospitals’ Accreditation especially in the area of data collection, analysis and regular audits of the various units of the hospital. Lawumi is passionate about Health Management and more importantly improving the Quality of healthcare delivery in Nigeria. She is a certified trainer and has facilitated several workshops and training programs in quality and patient safety.
Dr Oluborode in 2019 successfully spearheaded the rigorous process of submitting SQHN Healthcare standards for accreditation by the International Society for Quality in Healthcare (ISQua now IEAA) to firmly cement SQHN’s place among the world’s elite international accreditation organizations. She has received several awards for her excellent delivery of topics on healthcare quality and patient safety.
She obtained Msc Public Health (Health Management) from University of Lagos, a Certificate from University of Washington in Leadership & Management and graduated as a medical doctor in September 2004 with over 11years experience in healthcare Quality and Patient Safety

Governor 3 - Richard Ladapo

Richard Ladapo, is a graduate of Pharmacy from the Faculty of Pharmacy, University of Ibadan Lagos, and obtained a postgraduate diploma from the Centre for Drug Discovery, Development and Production (CDDDP), University of Ibadan in Drug Discovery and Development. He joined Evans Medical Plc (formerly a subsidiary of Glaxo, a multi-national pharmaceutical company that metamorphosed into GlaxoSmithKline) as a production pharmacist responsible for the operations of the manufacturing facility. He migrated to the Quality and Research department of the company as the Quality Compliance Manager responsible for the Regulatory and Quality Assurance operations of the company. He later joined Fidson Healthcare Plc from where he was seconded to Ecomed Pharma Ltd (a foremost contract manufacturing company based in Nigeria specializing pharmaceutical products) as the Quality Assurance Manager. He played different roles (Production Manager, Superintendent Pharmacist, Sales and Marketing Manager) before moving to Neros Pharmaceuticals Ltd as the founding Superintendent Pharmacist & Production Manager where he was responsible for the production and regulatory operations of the manufacturing facility.
A competent and experienced pharmacist with a proven track record of expertise in pharmaceutical services, highly skilled and certified in Drug Discovery and Development, Regulatory Pharmacy, Production Management, Quality Assurance and Control, he represented the pharmaceutical industry on the UNIDO/NAFDAC team for the Nigerian GMP Road Map and currently serve as the financial secretary of the Association of Industrial Pharmacists of Nigeria.
