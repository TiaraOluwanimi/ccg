Scholars Community Group

The Covenant Nation Scholars Community Group comprises undergraduates in Institutions of Higher Learning. The Scholar Community seeks to explore potentials inherent in undergraduates who are determined in their quest for academic excellence while creating an environment that will generate ideas relevant for nurturing participants into achieving their academic goals.
